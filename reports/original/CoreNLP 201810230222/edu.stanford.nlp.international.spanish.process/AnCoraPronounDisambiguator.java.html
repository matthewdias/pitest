/**
15 
 * A utility for preprocessing the AnCora Spanish corpus.
16

17 
 * Attempts to disambiguate Spanish personal pronouns which have
18 
 * multiple senses:
19 
 *
20 
 *     <em>me, te, se, nos, os</em>

 *
22
